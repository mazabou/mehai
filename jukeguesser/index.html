What is Jukebox

Jukebox is a neural network developed by OpenAI that was trained on a dataset of millions of songs to generate music in a variety of styles and genres.

How to play

Listen to the music generated by Jukebox in the style of a hidden artist, and try to guess the artist. Try to get the highest combo by making multiple consecutive correct guesses.

If you choose to skip the song or reveal the artist, the combo is reset.

Challenge friends

Need help or want to test your friends on a song you just guessed correctly? Share the link to the song with your friends!
